Project

NEW EXHUMATION PHASE FOR GRAVES AT BLOCKS 3A, 3B, 3C, 3D, 3E, 14E, 14F, 14G, 17, 18 AND 19 WITHIN THE CHOA CHU KANG CHINESE CEMETERY

In 1998, the Government announced the burial period for all graves within Choa Chu Kang (CCK) Cemetery would be limited to 15 years to ensure the sustainability of cemetery land use. Hence, graves that are 15 years or older would be progressively exhumed over time to free up land to allow development of new burial plots. Since 2004, CCK graves have been progressively exhumed, in phases.

The affected graves under CCK Phase 9 exhumation programme are located at BLOCKS 3A, 3B, 3C, 3D, 3E, 14E, 14F, 14G, 17, 18 and 19 within the CCK Chinese Cemetery (please refer to location map).
